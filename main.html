Giant Panda Facts

  1. Pandas are native to south central China.
  2. They primarily eat bamboo — about 99% of their diet.
  3. A newborn panda is about the size of a stick of butter.
  4. Despite their size, pandas are excellent climbers.
  5. Pandas spend 10–16 hours a day eating bamboo.
  6. Their distinctive black-and-white fur helps with camouflage.
  7. Pandas have a "pseudo thumb" to grip bamboo.
